Our Mission

We have a saying around here that you "pay for the atmosphere, and get the Kat Kutz for free." That's more the just lip-service; it's our reason for being. We understand and respect the immense amount of trust you place in   us.

From beginning to end, it's all about Katification® at Kat Kutz, which means that in addition to receiving a one-of-a-kind grooming experience, your furry friends will enjoy an environment that caters to their natural instincts to climb, perch, rest, play and own their space.We don't want to be the only place that grooms your cat; we simply want to be the   best

Services

Exotic Pets

We offer specialized care for reptiles, rodents, birds, and other exotic pets.

Grooming

Our therapeutic grooming treatments help battle fleas, allergic dermatitis, and other challenging skin conditions.

GeneralHealth

Wellness and senior exams, ultrasound, x-ray, and dental cleanings are just a few of our general health services.
